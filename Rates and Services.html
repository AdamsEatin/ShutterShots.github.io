Weddings

For enquiries about weddings please contact me through the form found on the Contact page.

The wedding package typically includes a full day of shooting, before, during and after the wedding. Photos will be processed and you have a choice of options as to how to present them, ranging from having files on a disk for your own personal use up to a photo book prepared with your favorite photos from the day.
