Welcome!

As a student I started this business in the hopes to offer people professional portraits at an affordable price. The Studio is located at 10 Harcourt street, Dublin 2.
CD Photo rights are available. Included in the CD option you get all the photographs as well as a copyright release form so you can take and print the photos at any location of your choice. You can also upload them to your computer and print unlimited copies without having to pay for costly packages. All photos are edited without any extra charge.
